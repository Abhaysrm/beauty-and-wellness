BEAUTY THERAPIST

A beauty therapist is a cosmetic professional who has training in a wide range of face and body treatments that can help clients enhance their natural beauty, maintain their appearance and improve how they feel. They may handle the same tasks as nail technicians, aestheticians, massage therapists and other beauty and wellness professionals.

These professionals are valuable members of the beauty industry as they can perform an array of services and work in a variety of professional environments. They may choose a niche within the industry to specialize in, depending on their training and interests.

What Beauty Therapists do?

A beauty therapist's responsibilities may vary depending on their employers, specializations and type of training they've received. Some of the common job duties a beauty therapist has includes:

  • analyzing their clients' skin condition to determine what kind of skincare or cosmetic treatment they require
  • creating personalized beauty processes for their clients and recommending them the right products
  • providing manicures and pedicures to clients and upselling their treatments
  • providing relaxing treatments for their clients, including massages or body wraps
  • using specialized equipment or products to conduct hair removal
  • adding eyelash extensions for their clients or shaping their eyebrows
  • conducting makeup looks for clients for various events or occasions
  • selling additional products or services during their beauty treatments
  • cleaning and sterilizing beauty equipment and the area before and after their treatments.

What services does a beauty therapist provide?

A beauty therapist typically specializes in a variety of beauty and cosmetic services. Some of their common services include:

  • microdermabrasion procedures
  • facial spas
  • chemical peels
  • light therapy treatments
  • laser resurfacing procedures
  • massages, including hot stone massages, lymphatic massages and slimming massages
  • hair removal procedures, whether temporary or permanent
  • manicures and pedicures
  • aromatherapy treatments
  • moisturizing treatments
  • eyebrow and eyelash beautifying treatments
  • makeup services
