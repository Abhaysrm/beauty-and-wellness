HAIR COURSE

DESCRIPTION: A hair designer is known to play the significant role of making their clients look stunningly unique and powerful by experimenting with their hair. A diploma in hair designing from PRIME ACADEMY arms you with the insight and skills required to spearhead this profession with aplomb.

The course offered by PRIME ACADEMY is ideal for both novice as well as experienced hairdressers and stylists who want to add extra miles to their careers. Our courses are designed to help you master on-trend and contemporary hairstyles while giving you enough leeway to add your own creative touch.

KNOWLEDGE: Students enrolling into our course are required to complete the entire duration of the course for certification. The classes will be conducted by experienced faculties and will include extensive hands-on practicals alongside insightful theory and demonstrations related to hair-care. You’ll be trained to master a plethora of haircuts, styling, and hair-coloring techniques. You’ll also learn about the nitty-gritty details of managing your own salon.

OPPORTUNITY: You can expect to launch a highly-rewarding career for yourself as a hair designer with IICTN’s diploma course. With a diploma in hair-designing in your bag, you’ll be able to:

  • Explore job opportunities at leading hair salons in the country.
  • Become a freelance or professional hair stylist/designer in the film or entertainment industry.
  • You can start your own hair salon or parlor.
ADVICE: We strongly advise against rushing to finish this course or pursuing it only for a certificate. Hair-designing is a skill-based subject that must not be undertaken for certification alone. We suggest completing a minimum of 150 hours of training with due diligence to get the most benefit out of it.
